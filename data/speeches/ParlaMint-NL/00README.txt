         Citation, documentation, download, and licence available from
                        http://hdl.handle.net/11356/2004

This directory contains the ParlaMint/TEI encoded version of the ParlaMint-NL
corpus. The root file ParlaMint-NL.xml contains the corpus teiHeader and
XIncludes of the component files, which have the form ParlaMint-NL_<suffix>.xml.
